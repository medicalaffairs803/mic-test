Frequently Asked Questions

How do I test my microphone online?

Click "Start Test", allow microphone access when your browser asks, then speak into your mic. The waveform will move if your mic is working. You'll get a quality score after stopping the test.

Why is my mic not working?

Check browser permissions — look for a mic icon in your address bar and make sure it's allowed. Also check that your mic isn't muted in Windows or Mac system settings.

Is this mic test free?

Yes, the basic mic test is completely free. Advanced diagnostics and AI suggestions require completing a free offer to unlock.

Which browsers work with this mic tester?

Chrome and Edge work best. Firefox also works well. Safari has limited support. Internet Explorer is not supported.
